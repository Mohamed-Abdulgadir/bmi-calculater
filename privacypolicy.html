Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 15, 2025

Important Note: This BMI Calculator app is designed to operate entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

Our BMI Calculator app is designed with privacy as a top priority. Here's what we collect:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as name, email, phone number, or location.
  • No Health Data Storage: Your height, weight, and BMI calculations are processed locally on your device and are not stored or transmitted.
  • No Analytics: We do not use analytics services to track your usage patterns or behavior.
  • No Third-Party Data Sharing: We do not share any data with third parties because we don't collect any data to share.

2. How Your Data is Processed

The BMI Calculator works entirely offline:

  • All calculations happen locally on your device
  • No internet connection is required for basic functionality
  • No data is sent to any external servers
  • Your input values (height and weight) exist only temporarily during the calculation process

3. Data Storage and Retention

We do not store any data. The app operates on a "calculate and display" model:

  • Input values are used only for immediate calculation
  • Results are displayed and not saved permanently
  • No history of calculations is maintained
  • When you close the app, all temporary data is cleared

4. App Permissions

The BMI Calculator app requires minimal permissions:

  • Network Access: Only used if you choose to share results (optional feature)
  • Storage: Minimal access for app installation and basic operation
  • No Location Services: The app does not access your location
  • No Contacts Access: The app does not access your contacts
  • No Camera/Microphone: The app does not access camera or microphone

5. Children's Privacy

Our BMI Calculator app does not collect any personal information from users of any age, including children under 13. Since no data is collected, the app is compliant with children's privacy protection regulations by design.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

7. Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Through the app's feedback mechanism (if available)
  • Via the app store listing contact information

8. Health Information Disclaimer

Important: This BMI Calculator app is for informational purposes only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.

9. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any user data, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to personal information through our app. Your privacy is protected by our "no data collection" approach.

10. International Users

This privacy policy applies to all users of the BMI Calculator app, regardless of their location. Since we do not collect or transfer any personal data, international data transfer regulations do not apply to our app.
